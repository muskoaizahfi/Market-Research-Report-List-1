<p><strong>The Prefabricated Building System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton System</li><li>Panel System</li><li>Cellular System</li><li>Combined System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefabricated building system market comprises four primary types: </p><p>1. **Skeleton System** - Uses a framework of beams and columns to support floors and walls, facilitating flexible space usage.  </p><p>2. **Panel System** - Involves pre-made panels for walls and roofs, allowing for quick assembly and efficient insulation.  </p><p>3. **Cellular System** - Features modular units, typically for multi-story constructions, promoting uniformity and easy expansion.  </p><p>4. **Combined System** - Merges elements from the other systems, optimizing construction processes for specific project needs.</p></p>
