<p><strong>The Diamond Cutting Tool Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Indexable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond cutting tool market is primarily categorized into solid and indexable types. Solid diamond tools are made from a single piece of diamond material, providing enhanced durability for precision applications in hard materials. In contrast, indexable diamond tools feature replaceable cutting inserts, allowing for quick changes and reduced downtime. Both types cater to various industries, such as aerospace and automotive, offering superior cutting performance and surface finishes while optimizing production efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
