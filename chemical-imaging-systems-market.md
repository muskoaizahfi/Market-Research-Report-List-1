Confocal Raman microscopes utilize Raman scattering to obtain chemical composition data at high resolution. Atomic force microscopes offer surface topography and mechanical properties at the nanoscale. Dispersive microscopes capture spectral data through dispersion techniques. Other market segments include hybrid systems and emerging technologies that enhance chemical imaging capabilities across various applications.</p></p>
