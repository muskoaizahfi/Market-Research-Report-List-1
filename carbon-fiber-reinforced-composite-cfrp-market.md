The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **PAN Matrix Composites** utilize polyacrylonitrile as the matrix for superior performance and versatility. </p><p>2. **Pitch Matrix Composites** leverage pitch-based matrices, offering high thermal and conductivity attributes. </p><p>3. **Resin Matrix Composites** employ epoxy or other resins, providing excellent adhesion and moldability. </p><p>4. **Others** encompass niche composites like thermoplastic or metal matrix CFRPs. Each type caters to specific applications across industries.</p></p>
