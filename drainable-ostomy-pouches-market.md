<p><p>The drainable ostomy pouch market features two primary types: one-piece and two-piece bags. One-piece bags combine the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, offering convenience and ease of use, suitable for those with active lifestyles. In contrast, two-piece bags consist of a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for easier changes and varied pouch options while minimizing skin irritation. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing comfort and maintaining hygiene for individuals with ostomies.</p></p>
