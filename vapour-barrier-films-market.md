<p><p>The vapor barrier films market consists of various types including sheet films, fluid-applied films, peel-and-stick films, and laminated vapor barrier films. Sheet films are rigid sheets laid over surfaces to prevent moisture ingress. Fluid-applied films are liquid solutions applied to create a seamless barrier. Peel-and-stick films offer user-friendly installation with adhesive backing, while laminated vapor barrier films combine multiple layers for enhanced durability and moisture resistance. These options cater to diverse construction and insulation needs, enhancing energy efficiency and preventing damage.</p></p>
