<p><p>Electromagnetic flowmeters are devices that measure the flow rate of conductive liquids using electromagnetic induction principles. They operate based on Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction, where a conductive fluid passing through a magnetic field generates a voltage proportional to its flow velocity. These flowmeters are widely used in various industries, including water treatment, chemical processing, and food and beverage, due to their accuracy, reliability, and ability to handle various fluid types without obstructing flow.</p><p>The Electromagnetic Flowmeters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.70% during the forecast period.
