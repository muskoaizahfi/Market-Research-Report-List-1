<p><p>Auto tempered glass is a type of safety glass that is created through a process of extreme heating and rapid cooling, making it stronger and more durable than regular glass. When shattered, auto tempered glass breaks into small, blunt pieces, reducing the risk of injury.</p><p>The Auto Tempered Glass Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing automotive production, technological advancements, and rising consumer awareness regarding vehicle safety.
