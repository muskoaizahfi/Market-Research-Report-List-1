<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HTS microplate readers are essential tools in high-throughput screening (HTS) applications, categorized into two market types: liquid and solid. Liquid microplate readers analyze samples in liquid formats, commonly used for assays involving solutions, such as enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and cell-based assays. Solid microplate readers, on the other hand, are designed for detecting reactions on solid surfaces, often utilized in applications like solid-phase assays and biosensing. Both types enhance efficiency in drug discovery and biomedical research.</p></p>
