<p><p>The hydrophobic intraocular lens market is divided into two primary types: double focus and three focus lenses. Double focus lenses offer two distinct focal points, typically for near and distance vision, allowing patients to see clearly at these ranges. In contrast, three focus lenses provide an additional focal point, often for intermediate vision, enhancing versatility for activities like reading or using a computer. Both types aim to improve visual acuity while minimizing dependence on glasses after cataract surgery.</p></p>
