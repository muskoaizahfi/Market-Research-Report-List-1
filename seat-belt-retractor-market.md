<p><ul><li>Single Stage (1-Stage) has 1 plug connector</li><li>Dual Stage (2-Stage) has 2plugs connectors</li><li>Triple Stage (3-Stage) has 3plugs connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Seat Belt Retractor market is segmented based on the number of stages, which corresponds to the complexity and functionality of the retractor mechanism. Single Stage retractors utilize one plug connector, providing basic functionality. Dual Stage retractors, equipped with two plug connectors, offer enhanced safety features, such as load limiting. Triple Stage retractors, featuring three plug connectors, provide advanced systems that can include additional features like pretensioners and improve overall passenger protection. Each type caters to different vehicle safety needs.</p></p>
