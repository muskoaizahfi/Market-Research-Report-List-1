<p><p>The cordless telephone market can be categorized into three main types: Analog, DECT, and other digital technologies. Analog cordless phones use traditional radio signals for communication, offering basic functionality but limited range and sound quality. DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) provides superior audio quality, extended range, and secure transmission, making it the preferred choice for many users. Other digital technologies, including Wi-Fi and Bluetooth-based systems, offer enhanced features such as voicemail integration and smartphone connectivity, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
