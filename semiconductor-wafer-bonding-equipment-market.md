<p><p>The semiconductor wafer bonding equipment market includes various types, notably wire bonders and die bonders. Wire bonders are used to create electrical connections between semiconductor devices and packaging using fine, metallic wires. They are essential for ensuring reliable electrical performance. Die bonders, on the other hand, attach semiconductor die to substrates or packages, providing mechanical support and electrical connectivity. Both equipment types play critical roles in the manufacturing and assembly processes of integrated circuits, driving advancements in semiconductor technology.</p></p>
