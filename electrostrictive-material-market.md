The market can be segmented into two main types: lead magnesium niobate-lead titanate (PMN-PT) and polymers. PMN-PT is a ceramic composite known for its high electrostrictive performance and is commonly used in sensors and actuators. Polymer electrostrictive materials, often lighter and more flexible, find applications in flexible electronics and biomedical devices, catering to diverse industries and innovative technologies.</p></p>
