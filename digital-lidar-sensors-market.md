<p><strong>The Digital Lidar Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Distance</li><li>Mid Distance</li><li>Long Distance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital LiDAR sensors are categorized based on their operational range: short distance, mid distance, and long distance. Short-distance LiDAR sensors typically cover up to a few hundred meters, ideal for applications like indoor mapping and small-scale projects. Mid-distance sensors range from several hundred meters to a few kilometers, suitable for urban planning and forestry. Long-distance LiDAR sensors can reach several kilometers, used extensively in topographic surveys, transportation planning, and large-scale environmental monitoring, providing high-resolution data over vast areas.</p></p>
