<p><strong>The Automatic External Defibrillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automated</li><li>Fully Automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic External Defibrillator (AED) market is predominantly categorized into semi-automated and fully automated devices. Semi-automated AEDs require the user to deliver a shock after the device analyzes the heart rhythm, providing prompts throughout the process. In contrast, fully automated AEDs take over the entire process, automatically delivering a shock without user intervention once a shockable rhythm is detected. Both types aim to enhance emergency response and increase survival rates during cardiac arrest incidents, catering to varying user expertise levels.</p></p>
