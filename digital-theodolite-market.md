<p><p>The digital theodolite market consists of two main types: optical and electronic theodolites. In contrast, electronic theodolites incorporate digital displays and advanced sensors, allowing for automated measurements and enhanced accuracy. This digital technology often integrates features like data storage, connectivity options, and graphic displays, making electronic theodolites more suitable for modern surveying tasks and improving efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
