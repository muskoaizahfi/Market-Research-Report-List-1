<p><p>Natriuretic peptides are hormones produced by the heart that play a critical role in regulating blood pressure and fluid balance. They are particularly important in the diagnosis and management of heart failure, as their levels can indicate cardiac stress and dysfunction. The primary natriuretic peptides include A-type Natriuretic Peptide (ANP) and B-type Natriuretic Peptide (BNP), which facilitate the excretion of sodium and promote vasodilation, providing therapeutic benefits in cardiovascular conditions.</p><p>The natriuretic peptide market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases and the rising demand for diagnostic tools in heart failure management. The market is characterized by advancements in biotechnology, which are leading to the development of more sensitive and specific assays for measuring natriuretic peptide levels. Innovations in point-of-care testing and home-based care are also shaping market trends, making diagnostics more accessible.</p><p>The Natriuretic Peptide Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period, reflecting an expanding focus on preventive healthcare and improved patient outcomes.
