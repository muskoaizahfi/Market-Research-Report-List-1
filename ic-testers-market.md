<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC testers are essential for ensuring the functionality of integrated circuits, and they primarily fall into two market types: wafer testers and packaged device testers. Wafer testers evaluate chips while they are still on the semiconductor wafer, allowing for high-throughput testing before packaging. In contrast, packaged device testers assess ICs after they have been encapsulated in their final form, focusing on performance and reliability. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining quality and efficiency in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
