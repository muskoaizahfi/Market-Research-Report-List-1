<p><ul><li>Water Based Adhesive</li><li>Solvent Based Adhesives</li><li>Hot Melt Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper converting adhesives market comprises three primary types: water-based adhesives, solvent-based adhesives, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, offering excellent bonding while minimizing volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-based adhesives contain higher levels of solvents, providing strong adhesion and fast drying but with greater health and environmental concerns. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that bond quickly upon cooling, offering versatility and ease of application in various paper converting processes like packaging and labeling.</p></p>
