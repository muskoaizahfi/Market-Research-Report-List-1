<p><p>The anti-diabetic drugs market includes several key classes: Biguanides, primarily metformin, which enhance insulin sensitivity; Sulfonylureas that stimulate insulin secretion; Meglitinides, offering rapid insulin release post-meals; Thiazolidinediones, improving insulin sensitivity and reducing resistance; Dipeptidyl Peptidase IV Inhibitors, enhancing incretin hormone effects to lower glucose; and α-Glucosidase Inhibitors, delaying carbohydrate absorption. Each class addresses different aspects of diabetes management, catering to various patient needs and helping in controlling blood sugar levels effectively.</p></p>
