<p><p>Hydraulic buffers can be categorized into two main market types: **tap** and **vertical**. Tap hydraulic buffers are designed for applications requiring precise energy absorption in machinery that operates horizontally, often utilized in conveyor systems or material handling. Vertical hydraulic buffers, on the other hand, are optimized for vertical movements, such as in elevators or lifting equipment. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing safety, reducing wear on mechanical components, and ensuring smooth operation across various industrial applications.</p></p>
