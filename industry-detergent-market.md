<p><p>The industrial detergent market encompasses various types, each serving distinct cleaning needs. Surfactants reduce surface tension, enhancing cleaning efficiency. Acids tackle mineral deposits and rust, while bases effectively remove grease and organic stains. Strong oxidants are powerful agents for disinfecting and bleaching. Enzymes target specific stains, such as proteins or fats, breaking them down for easier removal. Other categories may include specialty chemicals or additives designed for niche applications, expanding the versatility of industrial cleaning solutions.</p></p>
