<p><p>The turbine oil additives market comprises two main types: single component additives and additive packages. Single component additives are individual chemical substances that enhance specific properties of turbine oils, such as anti-wear, oxidation stability, or rust prevention. Additive packages, on the other hand, are pre-formulated blends containing multiple additives designed to provide a comprehensive solution for turbine oil performance. These packages simplify formulation processes and ensure compatibility, offering improved protection and efficiency for turbine operations.</p></p>
