<p><p>The radar chip market encompasses various types, including millimeter wave radar chips, which utilize high-frequency electromagnetic waves for precise sensing and imaging applications, typically in automotive and industrial settings. Ultrasonic radar chips, on the other hand, emit sound waves to detect objects and measure distance, commonly used in short-range applications like parking sensors. Lidar chips utilize laser light to create detailed three-dimensional maps of environments, crucial for autonomous vehicles and robotics. Each type serves distinct applications based on their sensing capabilities.</p></p>
