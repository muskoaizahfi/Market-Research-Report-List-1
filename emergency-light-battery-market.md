<p><p>The emergency light battery market features several key types. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform in extreme temperatures but have a lower energy density. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer higher capacity and are more environmentally friendly. Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries are lightweight, offer high energy density, and have longer life cycles, making them increasingly popular. Lead-acid batteries are cost-effective and reliable but heavier, making them suitable for larger emergency lighting systems. Each type caters to specific applications and user preferences.</p></p>
