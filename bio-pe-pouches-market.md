<p><p>The Bio PE Pouches market comprises three primary types based on filling methods: Hot Fill, Cold Fill, and Ambient Fill. Hot Fill pouches are designed to withstand high temperatures during filling, making them ideal for products like sauces and juices. Cold Fill pouches are used for beverages that are processed at lower temperatures, preserving freshness. Ambient Fill pouches are suitable for shelf-stable products, allowing for longer storage without refrigeration. Each type caters to specific product needs and shelf-life requirements.</p></p>
