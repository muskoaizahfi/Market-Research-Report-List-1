<p><strong>The Small Wind Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small wind turbine market is primarily divided into two types: Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical-Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs, characterized by their blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, are widely used due to their efficiency and higher energy output, especially in areas with consistent wind. In contrast, VAWTs feature blades that rotate around a vertical axis, offering advantages in turbulent wind conditions and requiring less maintenance, making them suitable for residential and urban applications.</p></p>
