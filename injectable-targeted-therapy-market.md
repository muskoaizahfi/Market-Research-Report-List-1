<p><p>The injectable targeted therapy market includes various types of treatment modalities designed to selectively target specific cancer cells or disease pathways. Monoclonal antibodies are lab-engineered proteins that bind to specific antigens on cancer cells, flagging them for destruction. Small molecules are low molecular weight compounds that interfere with cellular processes. Immunotoxins combine antibodies with toxic agents to directly kill cancer cells. Other therapies may include peptide-based therapeutics and gene therapies, providing diverse options for tailored disease management.</p></p>
