<p><strong>The Anti-static Overalls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split Type</li><li>Siamese Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-static overalls market can be categorized into two main types: split type and Siamese type. Split type overalls feature a design that allows the upper and lower parts to be separated, enhancing flexibility and ease of wear. In contrast, Siamese type overalls are a one-piece construction, providing comprehensive coverage and reduced risk of static discharge. Both types cater to industries where controlling static electricity is crucial, such as electronics manufacturing and cleanroom environments, ensuring worker safety and product integrity.</p></p>
