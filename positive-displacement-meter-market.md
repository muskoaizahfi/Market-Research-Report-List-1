<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Gear Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The positive displacement meter market is categorized into three main types: diaphragm type, gear type, and others. Diaphragm type meters utilize flexible membranes to measure fluid flow, offering high accuracy for low flow rates. Gear type meters use rotating gears that trap and measure fluid, known for their robustness and suitability in various applications. The "others" category includes various specialized meters, such as sliding vane and rotary output types, catering to specific industry needs and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
